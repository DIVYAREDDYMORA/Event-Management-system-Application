Paris Haute Couture
Date: Monday, June 24, 2024, to Thursday, June 27, 2024
Venue: Paris

Paris Haute Couture is a bi-annual event held in Paris, France, that showcases top fashion shows and the latest haute couture collections from some of the world’s most prestigious design houses. Haute couture, which translates to “high sewing” or “high dressmaking,” refers to custom, made-to-order garments for a specific client. The event is highly anticipated in the fashion industry and is known for its opulent and luxurious fashion. Participants include Chanel, Christian Dior, Valentino, Elie Saab, Gaurav Gupta, Fendi Couture, and other popular labels showcasing their clothes, accessories and designer handbags.
